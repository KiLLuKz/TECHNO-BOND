# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Seniors and Juniors within the school environment. They use this platform as a hub to connect, complete missions, and track their progress.

## Product Purpose
A gamified dashboard system that serves as a central hub for student interactions, missions, and progression tracking.

## Brand Personality
Cyberpunk, Futuristic, Professional

## Anti-references
- Hard-to-use interfaces or cluttered layouts
- Emoji-heavy designs that make it look unprofessional or juvenile
- Light themes (must remain strictly dark/neon)

## Design Principles
- **Function over form:** It must be easy to use despite the complex aesthetic.
- **Strictly Professional Cyberpunk:** Keep the neon and dark mode vibes without devolving into playful or childish visuals (no emojis).
- **Clear Information Architecture:** Data and actions must be unambiguous and immediately recognizable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for text against dark backgrounds (neon accents should not compromise readability).
- Clear focus states and easy navigation for all users.
